Usage Guidelines
⚫
Dark Version
  • Digital screens, presentations
  • Social media banners & posts
  • Event backdrops & LED walls
  • Video titles & motion graphics
  • Email headers (dark mode)
  • Website hero sections
⚪
Light Version
  • Printed documents & reports
  • Proposals & quotations
  • Letterheads & invoices
  • Business cards (white BG)
  • Print advertising
  • Document cover pages
🔲
Icon Mark
  • Social media profile pictures
  • App icons & favicons
  • Watermarks on event photos
  • Merchandise & branded gear
  • Corner badge on all docs
  • WhatsApp / Teams display
📐
Clear Space Rule
  • Minimum clear space = height of "B" in BW
  • Never place on busy photographic backgrounds
  • Never stretch or skew proportions
  • Never recolour the BW monogram
  • Never remove the flame ring
🎨
Do's
  • Use gold (#C9A84C) for body text accents
  • Use Cinzel serif for display headings
  • Use Inter for body copy
  • Spectrum dividers between sections
  • Black or white backgrounds only
🚫
Don'ts
  • No grey or coloured backgrounds
  • No logo below 40px wide
  • No drop shadows added externally
  • No low-resolution exports below 300dpi (print)
  • No unapproved colour variants
